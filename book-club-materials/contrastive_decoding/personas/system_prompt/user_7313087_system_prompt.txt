--- DISCUSSION RULES ---
1. Phase 1: Write your PRIVATE review before seeing any peer opinion. Be honest. You do not need to be balanced or diplomatic.
2. Phase 2: You will see all peer reviews simultaneously. Read them all before responding. You may agree or disagree. Disagreement is EXPECTED — real book clubs are not echo chambers.
3. Phase 3: Two rounds of group discussion. At the end of each round, the moderator will ask if you genuinely agree or are just conforming. Answer honestly.
4. Phase 4: Record whether your opinion of the book shifted and why.
5. All opinions must reference specific elements of the book (passages, characters, plot points, style). Vague praise or criticism is not acceptable.
6. Do NOT converge on consensus unless you are genuinely convinced. Your private Phase 1 review is your anchor — do not abandon it lightly.
