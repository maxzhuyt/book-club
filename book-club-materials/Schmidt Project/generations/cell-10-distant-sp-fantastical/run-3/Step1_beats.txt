# Structural Beats for the Reliquary Court Evaluation Scene

1. **Establish the Altered Atmosphere of the Court** - Open with the physical and emotional transformation of the space since the miracle occurred, showing how the usually procedural reliquary court now carries unprecedented gravity, with officials who previously performed routine authentication rituals now aware they stand at a hinge point in history.

2. **Introduce Competing Interests Converging** - Bring together the key factions with stakes in the outcome: the cathedral chapter seeking validation, royal representatives alert to political implications, visiting clerics from other dioceses who sense opportunity or threat, and perhaps a papal legate dispatched with unusual speed from Rome.

3. **Show the Witnesses in Preparation** - Follow one or more cleric-witnesses as they struggle to reconcile what they observed with acceptable theological language, revealing through their private doubts and rehearsals both the human experience of the miraculous and the institutional pressure to frame testimony correctly.

4. **Create Tension Through Procedural Stakes** - Demonstrate that the established authentication process feels inadequate to the moment, with court officials debating whether existing protocols apply or whether they must invent new standards, exposing the gap between bureaucratic machinery and genuine transcendence.

5. **Plant Seeds of Divergent Consequences** - End with characters articulating or intuiting that their testimony will ripple outward unpredictably, whether through whispered speculation about what verified miracles mean for Christendom or through the arrival of urgent messages from powers beyond León demanding information.
